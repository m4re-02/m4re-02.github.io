About Us

A connection between a beautiful ambient and atmosphere

O restoranu

The newly opened restaurant "Ambijent Gold", located next to the highway in Čačak, on the way to Ovčar Banja, offers you specialties based on traditional Serbian cuisine. Our kind and professional staff will make sure that your stay here is unforgettable and will exceed your expectations.

Great selection of food and drinks

We offer a great selection of top quality specialties from traditional Serbian cuisine, as well as an excellent range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks for everybody's taste.

Party organisation

We organise all kinds of partys in our hall for maximum of 60 guests, and our garden with a maximum of 40 guests. Contact Us for more information
